Add a Profile Completeness indicator to the Profile / CV page.

Goal:
Motivate workers to complete their professional profile.

Requirements:

1. Add a visible profile completion card near the profile preview section, preferably on the right side or directly under the preview card.

2. Show:
   - Completion percentage
   - Progress bar
   - Short motivational message
   - Missing items checklist

3. Calculate profile completion based on:

Profile photo uploaded: 10%
Full name completed: 5%
Current position completed: 5%
Current company completed: 5%
Location completed: 5%
Years of experience completed: 5%
Skills added: 10%
Professional bio completed: 10%
CV uploaded: 15%
At least 1 work experience added: 15%
At least 1 certification added: 10%
At least 1 professional document uploaded: 5%

Total: 100%

4. Checklist examples:
   - Add a profile photo
   - Upload your CV
   - Add your first work experience
   - Add at least one certification
   - Add professional skills
   - Complete your professional bio
   - Upload supporting documents

5. Use the existing PipingBox dark industrial design:
   - dark card
   - amber/yellow accent
   - subtle border
   - compact progress bar

6. Add status labels:
   - 0–39%: "Profile incomplete"
   - 40–69%: "Good start"
   - 70–89%: "Almost ready"
   - 90–100%: "Recruiter-ready profile"

7. In the Company Candidate Detail page, optionally show the candidate profile completion percentage as a small badge, but do not show the private checklist.

8. The calculation must use real existing data:
   - profile fields
   - uploaded CV fields
   - app_worker_experiences count
   - app_worker_certifications count
   - documents count if available

9. If documents table is not available, skip documents from the calculation or treat it as optional.

10. Do not create new tables.
11. Do not break existing Profile / CV, certifications, work experience, CV upload, or alert preferences.
12. Keep Spanish and English translations.
